History of Bank of Dheeraj

  • Bank of Dheeraj was originally promoted in 1997 , an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
  • The principal objective was to create a development financial institution for providing medium-term and long-term project financing to Indian businesses.
  • In 2000, it became the first Indian company and the first bank or financial institution from non-Japan Asia to be listed on the New York Stock Exchange.
  • Bank of Dheeraj offers a wide range of banking products and financial services to corporate and retail customers through a variety of delivery channels and through its group companies.

  • Bank of Dheeraj is deeply engaged in human and economic development at the national level. The Bank works closely
    with Government Foundation across diverse sectors and programs.
